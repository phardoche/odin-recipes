Preparation 20 minutes | Cooking time 3 hours 15 minutes | Quantity 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 5 ml fresh thyme
  • 125 ml dark beer
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 500 ml beef stock
  • 15 ml sliced garlic
  • 4 sliced onions
  • 45 ml maple sirup
  • 15 ml balsamic vinegar
  • 1 chuck roast (675gr or 1 1/2 lbs)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 180°C (350°F)
  2. Sponge the chuck roast and cover with salt and pepper.
  3. In a big oven safe pot, heat half the oil at medium heat.
    Cook the chuck roast for 2 to 3 minutes on each side, until golden.
    Reserve on a plate.
  4. In the same pot, heat the remaining oil at medium heat.
    Cook the onions for 10 to 12 minutes stirring frequently, till they caramelize.
  5. Add the maple sirup and the vinegar.
  6. Keep cooking stirring frequently till all liquid evaporated and that the onions get a golden color.
  7. Add the beer and simmer till half has evaporated.
  8. Put back the chuck roast in the pot, then add the stock, garlic and thyme.
  9. Cover with the lid and cook in the oven for 3 to 3 1/2 hours, till the meat easily shreds with a fork.
  10. Serve with potatoes and a salad.
