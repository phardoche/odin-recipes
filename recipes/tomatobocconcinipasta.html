Preparation 15 minutes | Cooking time 8 minutes | Quantity 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 350 gr gemelli or other short pasta
  • 175 gr Genoa Salami finely sliced
  • 1 garlic clover (or more to taste)
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes sliced in halves (or more to taste)
  • 2 green scalions or 1/4 cup of chives chopped
  • 1 pint pearl bocconcini or bigger (cut to taste)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pastas according to packaging.
  2. Prepare all ingredients and mix in a large bowl. Do not cook anything else then pastas.
  3. Drain pasta water and mix with the rest of ingredients.
  4. Adjust olive oil, salt and pepper to taste.
  5. For those who like feet taste, add gratted parmesan cheese.

Reheat, eat lukewarm or cold from the frigde.
